Lenneth's Spaghetti

Simplicity at its finest.

The traditional pasta Aglio & Olio.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Black Pepper
  • Optional: Red Chilli
  • Herbs
  • Parmesan Cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta as per the instructions in the box.
  2. In a frying pan, saut√© the garlic and red chilli until the garlic develops a little hint of color.
  3. Add a hefty batch of black pepper and mix the pasta in.
  4. Finish with some herbs and Parmesan cheese.

Tips

  • Do not be stingy with the olive oil, it binds the flavors of all ingredients into the pasta.
  • Shrimp or chicken breast can both be a nice addition to this dish.
  • Add a bit of butter at the end for a silky texture.
